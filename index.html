Our Mission

San Joaquin Valley Town Hall is a non-profit organization that is run by an all-volunteer board of directors. Our mission is to bring nationally and internationally renowned, thought-provoking speakers who inform, educate, and entertain our audience.

Featured Speaker

Scott Sampson

Dr. Scott Sampson is a renowned paleontologist and science communicator best known for his work on the PBS Kids show Dinosaur Train. Join us as he explores the fascinating connections between dinosaurs, evolution, and our modern world.
