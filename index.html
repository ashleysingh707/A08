Scott Sampson

What's 75 million years old and brand spanking new? A teenage Utahceratops! Come to the Saroyan Theatre and meet your favorite dinosaur roar when Scott Sampson, Research Curator at the Utah Museum of Natural History, steps to the podium. Sampson's research focuses on the ecology and evolution of late Cretaceous dinosaurs, and he has conducted fieldwork across Africa.
