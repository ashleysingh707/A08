Scott Sampson

Scott Sampson is a renowned paleontologist, author, and science communicator. He is known for his work in promoting nature education and inspiring curiosity about the natural world. Sampson has appeared on multiple TV programs and authored books aimed at connecting children and families with the wonders of science.
